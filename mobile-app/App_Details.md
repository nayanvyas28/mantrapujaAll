# Mantra Puja - App Overview

## Title
**Mantra Puja: Your Spiritual Path to Peace**

## Description
Mantra Puja is a comprehensive spiritual companion designed to help you align with cosmic energies and find inner peace. Rooted in Sanatan wisdom, the app provides a seamless bridge between ancient traditions and modern convenience, offering daily spiritual guidance, authentic puja services, and personalized astrological insights.

## Key Features

### 1. Daily Spiritual Guidance & Sanatan Wisdom
Receive daily quotes, teachings, and spiritual insights to start your day with mindfulness and positivity.

### 2. Authentic Puja Booking
Easily book authentic pujas performed by experienced pandits. Whether it's for prosperity, health, or special occasions, find the right spiritual support at your fingertips.

### 3. Personalized Daily Horoscope & Zodiac
Stay aligned with the stars with detailed daily horoscopes and personalized zodiac insights to understand the cosmic energies affecting your life.

### 4. Festival & Spiritual Calendar
Never miss an important date. The integrated calendar tracks upcoming festivals, important tithis, and spiritual events, keeping you connected to your traditions.

### 5. Numerology & Kundli Insights
Explore the deeper meaning of numbers in your life and gain insights through personalized numerological and kundli-based analyses.

### 6. Spiritual Blogs & Resources
Dive deep into Sanatan traditions, spiritual practices, and Vedic knowledge through a curated list of blogs and educational content.

### 7. Interactive Spiritual Chatbot
Get instant answers to your spiritual queries and guidance on various religious practices through our AI-integrated chatbot.

### 8. Notifications & Reminders
Set reminders for daily mantras, upcoming pujas, and festival celebrations so you always stay on your spiritual path.
